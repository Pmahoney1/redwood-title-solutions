How We Work

Our Curative
Process

Every title problem has a solution. We follow a disciplined, methodical process refined across hundreds of complex resolutions.

01

Discovery & Title Search

We begin with a comprehensive search — county records, court filings, tax rolls, and probate indices — to map every cloud on title and identify all parties with potential interest.

02

Risk Assessment

We analyze each defect, assess risk, and determine the most efficient curative strategy. Some clouds resolve quickly; others require more involved approaches. We map the full picture before committing.

03

Curative Action

From coordinating heirship affidavits and quiet title actions to managing lien negotiations and tax redemptions, our team drives the resolution strategy with precision — working alongside attorneys and title professionals.

04

Clear Title & Close

Once the clouds are removed, we coordinate final title review, underwriter approval, and a clean closing — delivering a fully insurable, marketable property ready for its next chapter.
