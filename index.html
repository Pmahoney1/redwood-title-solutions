What We Do

We Resolve What Others Cannot

Title problems are rarely simple. We bring deep expertise and creative problem-solving to the most tangled ownership situations.

Tax Delinquent Properties

We identify and pursue tax-delinquent properties, navigating the complex redemption process, unpaid tax liens, and delinquency auction systems to establish clear, insurable title.

  • Tax lien analysis & redemption
  • County tax auction support
  • Delinquency timeline resolution

Probate & Estate Title

When a property owner passes without proper probate, title becomes trapped. We work through heirship affidavits, intestate succession, and court proceedings to free the asset and establish clear ownership.

  • Heirship affidavit preparation
  • Intestate succession research
  • Probate court coordination

Litigation & Lien Resolution

Properties caught in lawsuits or burdened by multiple competing liens require specialized expertise. We untangle competing claims, negotiate settlements, and coordinate legal remedies to produce a clean chain of title.

  • Multi-lien priority analysis
  • Quiet title action coordination
  • Judgment lien negotiation

Title Search & Opinion

A thorough title search is the foundation of every transaction. Our team conducts deep-dive chain of title research and delivers comprehensive opinions that reveal every cloud — before it becomes a crisis.

  • Full chain of title research
  • Cloud identification
  • Attorney opinion letters

Investment Acquisitions

We partner with real estate investors seeking distressed assets with title complexity. Our team identifies, evaluates, and acquires these properties while simultaneously engineering the curative path that unlocks their value.

  • Distressed property sourcing
  • Title risk assessment
  • Joint venture structuring

Property Due Diligence

We conduct comprehensive property research and analysis to uncover hidden risks — giving investors and buyers the full picture before closing on any deal.

  • Chain of title verification
  • Encumbrance & lien discovery
  • Ownership history reports

How We Work

Our Curative
Process

Every title problem has a solution. We follow a disciplined, methodical process refined across hundreds of complex resolutions.

01

Discovery & Title Search

We begin with a comprehensive search — county records, court filings, tax rolls, and probate indices — to map every cloud on title and identify all parties with potential interest.

02

Risk Assessment

We analyze each defect, assess legal risk, and determine the most efficient curative strategy. Some clouds resolve quickly; others require litigation. We map the full picture before committing.

03

Curative Action

From heirship affidavits and quiet title actions to lien negotiations and tax redemptions, our team executes the resolution strategy with precision — working alongside your legal team or independently.

04

Clear Title & Close

Once the clouds are removed, we coordinate final title review, underwriter approval, and a clean closing — delivering a fully insurable, marketable property ready for its next chapter.
