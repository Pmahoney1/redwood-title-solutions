Who We Are

About Redwood Title Solutions

Our Story

Built on Strength. Rooted in Resolve.

Redwood Title Solutions was founded on a simple premise: the most challenging title problems are also the most valuable to solve. We are a specialized curative title group focused on properties where traditional buyers and title companies walk away.

Where others see risk and walk away, we see opportunity to deliver clarity. From tax-delinquent estates tangled in years of unpaid obligations, to probate gaps left by missing heirs, to properties buried under layers of competing liens and unresolved judgments — these are the cases that define our work.

Like the redwood — the tallest, most resilient trees on earth — we take root in difficult ground. Our team brings decades of combined experience in real estate investing, title research, and navigating complex property situations. We don't just identify title problems — we engineer solutions by coordinating with the right attorneys, title professionals, and specialists to get deals done.

Whether you are an heir trying to unlock a family property, a lender navigating a distressed asset, or a property owner facing clouds on your title that prevent sale or refinancing, we bring clarity where there was confusion — and we close.

Our Values

What Drives Us

Precision

Every recommendation we make is backed by primary source verification — county records, court filings, and public databases. We never guess. We research, verify, and confirm before we act.

Partnership

We operate as an extension of your team, not as a faceless vendor. You will always know the status of your case, and we will always tell you the hard truth — even when it is not what you want to hear.

Persistence

Curative title work demands tenacity. We pursue every lead, exhaust every option, and push relentlessly toward resolution. When others give up, we dig deeper.

Integrity

We are straightforward about timelines, costs, and expected outcomes. If a case has risks, we disclose them. If a path is unlikely to succeed, we say so upfront. Our reputation depends on honesty.

Our Approach

Service Philosophy

At Redwood Title Solutions, we believe that every property deserves a clear path to marketable title. Our approach combines rigorous research with creative problem-solving to navigate even the most complex title situations. We coordinate with attorneys, title companies, lenders, and other professionals to drive curative strategies tailored to each unique case. From initial title research through final resolution and closing, we provide end-to-end project management with transparent communication at every stage. We measure our success not just by cases resolved, but by the lasting relationships we build with the clients and communities we serve.
